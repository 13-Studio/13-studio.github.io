Full realization of music recordings

Recording

We offer sound recording in acoustically adapted and isolated rooms. Live Room with a space of 63 m2 and a height of 3.4 m. Vocal Booth with a space of 5.3 m2 and a height of 2.3 m. You can use a selection of high-class microphones and preamplifiers with the Apogee Symphony Mk II analog-digital converter.

Editing

We provide track processing in Logic Pro X with the ability to remove noise, adjust pitch, equalize, reamp, resample etc.

Mix/Mastering

We have a wide selection of equalizers, compressors and various types of effects in both, analog and digital forms. Everything summed by the legendary Sigma SSL, which gives the mixes a unique character. Our comfortable and spacious Control Room is equipped with professional studio monitors and Avantgarde audiophile columns ensuring attention to every detail.
